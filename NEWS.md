# spatialData 0.0.1

- Adds datasets `vi`, `neanderthal`, and `quercus`.
- Adds dataset `plantae`: plant diversity metrics (richness, beta diversity, rarity) for global terrestrial ecoregions.
- Adds dataset `trees`: tree species richness across a hexagonal grid spanning the Americas.
- Adds dataset `communities`: plant community records from the Sierra Nevada (Spain) with future climate scenarios for 2050 and 2100.
- Adds dataset `interaction`: butterfly-plant interaction data from the Sierra Nevada, modelling *Agriades zullichi* and its host plant *Androsace vitaliana*.
- Adds dataset `andalusia`: presence records of threatened and endemic plant species across Andalusia, Spain.
- Adds dataset `linaria`: presence records of the endemic plant *Linaria nigricans* alongside greenhouse land-use data in Eastern Andalusia.
